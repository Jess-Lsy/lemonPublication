Eligibility

Lemon Publication is committed to promoting and celebrating creative expression, literature, and the arts on our campus. To ensure the integrity and effectiveness of our mission, we have established the following eligibility criteria for individuals interested in becoming members of our organization: - Enrollment at Carnegie Mellon University: All members of Lemon Publication must be currently enrolled students at Carnegie Mellon University, including undergraduate and graduate students from all academic disciplines. - Diversity and Inclusivity: Lemon Publication welcomes students from all backgrounds, regardless of their race, gender, nationality, religion, sexual orientation, or any other characteristic. We are dedicated to creating an inclusive and welcoming environment for all, promoting DEI principles in all aspects of our activities. - Passion for Creative Expression: Prospective members should have a genuine interest in and enthusiasm for creative expression, including writing, visual arts, poetry, literature, and other artistic forms. We encourage a broad spectrum of creative interests and talents, valuing diverse perspectives. - Active Participation: Members are expected to actively engage in the activities and initiatives of Lemon Publication. This includes attending meetings, contributing to the publication, and assisting in organizing events. - Respect for Community Guidelines: All members are expected to adhere to the community guidelines set forth by Lemon Publication, which prioritize respect, professionalism, and ethical conduct, ensuring a safe and inclusive environment. Lemon Publication has a zero-tolerance policy for discrimination, harassment, or any form of harmful behavior towards fellow members. Respect and kindness, in alignment with DEI principles, are of utmost importance. Prospective members who meet these eligibility criteria are encouraged to get involved with Lemon Publication and join us in our mission to foster a vibrant creative community at Carnegie Mellon University. Please note that Lemon Publication reserves the right to amend these eligibility criteria as needed to ensure the continued growth and success of the organization. For more information on how to become a member, please visit our website, attend one of our information sessions, or contact our executive board for further details. We look forward to welcoming new members who are enthusiastic about the arts and share our dedication to creative expression.
