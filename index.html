Lemon

Lemon Publication

Lemon aims to showcase the talented writers, artists, performers, and designers at CMU, and allow for opportunity for their work with the community. We intend to build a community for the entire artistic population at CMU, as well as break the walls between art, design, architecture, music, etc. We want to create opportunities for the artistic population on campus to be showcased and celebrated.
